A year in the life of me

Posted: December 4, 2025

So we are getting close to the end of the year, this has been a year with a lot of change for me and i was thinking it would be a good idea to summarize it and look at some of the hight lightâ€™s.

2024 ended with me, together with my future wife, starting and finishing our first Hyrox race. I think this was something that left a big mark on the rest of the year and in some way was the start of all the change that 2025 actually brought.

Over the year I have struggled with not knowing if I would lose my sight or if any of the various injections I have received in my eye over the year would help. I have oscillated between hope and disappointment as I have tried several different preparations but none except the last one actually seem to help. I realise that my defence against disappointment has been that I have always set myself up for it not to work.

But it was a huge relief when I received the news in October that there was a positive change in my eye. I finally got the green light to get new glasses that suited my changed vision.

Married

My partner and I had been talking for a long time about getting married when it was getting closer to buying a house together, more like it would make everything a little easier legally. But then we started talking about why we were actually putting it off; it would be a big deal for my mom to be there and sheâ€™s not getting any younger. So we decided to do it this year and when we were looking at the plan we decided that the end of May was a perfect time and we made up our minds.

Changing job

I hadnâ€™t really had big plans to change jobs, but I was drawn into the feeling of being able to handle the tough challenge that Hyrox actually is and started looking around. I quickly got in touch with a consulting firm that felt right, I got a lucrative salary offer and I took the plunge. It then went by quickly before I had my first placement ready so when I left my last employer on Thursday, I got married on Friday and on Monday I started my new job.

I am now completely convinced that I made the right decision at the right time. I have been given the opportunity to further develop myself not only as an engineer but also as a team leader.

I prefer that title as I think it reflects what I do so much more than scrum master.

What to come

I really think that 2026 will be a very good year for me; I will continue to develop, I have a lot of goals and expectations for myself. We end the year the same way as last year; we will run one more Hyrox race. The goal is to get to the finish line in a better time than last year, next year I also want to continue to develop with my training. I have come a long way during the year but next year I will really put in an extra gear. To compete in at least two Hyrox races in 2026 is the plan - probably Copenhagen and Stockholm.
